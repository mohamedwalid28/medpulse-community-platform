# Strategic Analysis: Build vs. Buy

### The Recommendation: "Hybrid Launch"
To meet the goal of a fast, high-quality launch for the founder, I recommend:

1. **BUY (Circle.so) for Engagement:** 
   - Use Circle for the Feed, Chat, and Member Profiles to ensure a "World Class" UI on Day 1.
2. **BUILD (Django/Python) for Proprietary Logic:** 
   - **Video Library:** Custom indexing of the 80+ videos for HIPAA-compliant search.
   - **AI Summaries:** Custom Python logic to generate medical summaries of discussions.
   - **Automation:** FastAPI middleware to sync Circle members with the Practice CRM.
